About Addons DPS list

Addons are small helpful programs that you inject into your WoW when starting it up. These are made by players for players, that wanted to make it easier to play the game.

Addons go in to the folder: "...World of Warcraft\Interface\Addons\..."

(Info: If the folder is zipped/compressed you need to unzip it for it to work).
