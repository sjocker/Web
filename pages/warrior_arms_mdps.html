Intro:

Arms warriors in World of Warcraft's Wrath of the Lich King expansion (Patch 3.3.5) are a formidable melee damage dealer class, specializing in the use of two-handed weapons to deliver powerful, sweeping strikes to their enemies. They have a range of abilities at their disposal to enhance their damage output, including powerful strikes, debuffs that increase damage taken by their targets, and abilities that allow them to enter a battle stance, further increasing their damage. Arms warriors are a high-energy class, with a playstyle that emphasizes the use of their abilities to maintain a steady stream of damage. In addition to their damage-dealing capabilities, arms warriors also have a number of abilities that allow them to control the battlefield and assist their allies, making them a valuable member of any raid or dungeon group.
