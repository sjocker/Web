Death Knight -> Unholy -> mdps

Difficulty: Medium

Intro:

As an Unholy Melee DPS Death Knight in patch 3.3.5 of Wrath of the Lich King, you are a master of death and decay, capable of dealing devastating damage to your enemies while also being able to survive against their attacks. Your combination of powerful physical attacks, diseases, and control abilities make you a formidable opponent in both solo and group play. With the right gear and talent build, you are able to excel in a damage-dealing role in end-game content. Whether you are unleashing the power of your diseases on your enemies or striking them down with your deadly melee attacks, your mastery of the dark arts is a force to be reckoned with.
